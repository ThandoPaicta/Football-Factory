Welcome To Football Factory

About Footballers Factory



Footballers Factory is a registered Non-Profit Company and a talent sport development program or initiative where kids "Learn and have Fun." Our focus is to ensure that the youngsters are groomed at an early age and choose good role models. Our aim is to remove any barriers that might discourage them from reaching their utmost potential and create a position that will see them developed in a holistic manner.

Developing Young Talents

We focus on nurturing and guiding young talents to develop their skills and become leaders on and off the field.

Our Mission



  • Identify potential talented footballers.
  • Nurture and guide the participants to develop their skills.
  • Educate participants, giving them a competitive advantage through education.
  • Mentor participants through mentoring sessions and partner them with Mentors.
  • Instill good values that participants will adopt and live by.
  • Expose participants to new experiences and broaden their mindset while offering opportunities.
    •

Our Goals



  • To establish a local Football Club that will play in PSL and boast with local players.
  • To play a major role in shaping the youngsters during their childhood stage.
  • To create a hub where top clubs in the country depend on us for feeder talent in their youth teams.
  • To pursue academic excellence by providing assistance in extra curriculum activities and supporting education.
  • To link participants with companies (corporate, factories, etc.) for exposure to different career paths.
  • To produce well-informed and trendsetting coaches, administrators, facility managers, etc.

Our Services



At Footballers Factory, we are committed to providing exceptional services that empower and uplift young talents. Our vision for the future reflects our dedication to creating a pathway for success for all participants. Here are the services we offer:

Football Club Establishment

Our primary goal is to establish a local Football Club that will play in prestigious leagues like the PSL and proudly boast local players. We aim to provide a platform for these talented individuals to shine on the national and international stage.

Youth Talent Development

We understand the importance of nurturing young talents during their childhood stage. Our focus is on shaping these youngsters and guiding them towards becoming future leaders both on and off the field.

Sports Excellence

We aim to create a hub where top clubs in the country depend on us for feeder talent in their youth teams. Our commitment to sports excellence ensures that our participants are well-trained and prepared for success at the highest level.

Academic Support

Academic excellence is a priority for us. We provide assistance in extra-curricular activities, including homework, assignments, and any other activities prescribed by teachers. Our goal is to ensure our participants excel both in sports and academics.

Career Exposure

We link our participants with companies, corporate organizations, factories, and other industries. Through programs like "Take a Child to Work," we offer exposure to different career paths, helping them make informed decisions about their future.

Professional Development

We are dedicated to producing well-informed and trendsetting coaches, administrators, facility managers, and other sports professionals. Our focus is on creating a strong foundation for the future of sports management and leadership.



Vision


WHY US.

Footballers Factory envisions a future where we unearth and develop talent that can compete globally, if not at the highest leagues in the world. We strive to see our players participate in major competitions like the Champions League and excel in the world's greatest leagues. Our ultimate goal is to develop leaders and stable individuals who, in turn, will become developers and leaders for others in the future.
